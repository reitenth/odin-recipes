Pasta Salad

Pasta salad!

This is one of my favorite dish recipes. Pasta salad!

Ingredients

Vinaigrette Dressing

Rotini Noodles

Can of Olives

Red or Green Pepper

Mozzarella Cheese

Onion

Steps

1. Boil water and pasta on stove until desired texture, usually 10-12 minutes.

2. Cut up all your veggies into small dices.

3. When noodles are done, strain them and pour them into big bowl.

4. Mix all your veggies and dressing with the noodles and plate!
